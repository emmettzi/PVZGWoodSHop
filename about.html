About Us

We founded PVZGWoodShop because we grew up in Alaska, where everyone had a shop in their garage to build things for outside, the winter, or to defend against bears. When we moved here we realized that there are few people that have woodshops. We knew how fun it was, having grown up building stuff, so we created PVZGWoodShop Because it was what the people needed. We have been providing our space for a small fee to the community ever since.
